# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Giraê ("we," "us," "the bot," "the platform") collects when you use our Telegram/Discord bot and related services, how we use it, and what choices you have.

Giraê is a card-collecting game bot. Playing requires interacting with it through Telegram or Discord, and some data collection is unavoidable to make the game work (tracking your cards, trades, currency, and similar gameplay state).

---

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Account identifiers:** Your Telegram/Discord ID and username, so we know who you are across sessions.
- **Profile information:** Anything you choose to add to your profile (display name, avatar, bio, preferences, cosmetic choices, and similar customization), plus any status tied to your account, such as administrator or moderation status.
- **Gameplay data:** Records needed to run the game itself — things like your collection, currency, trades, wishlists, goals, and a history of in-game actions (e.g., draws, trades, purchases, promo code redemptions).
- **Administrative logs:** If you are staff, actions you take through admin tools are logged for accountability.
- **Staff login data:** Administrators who access our internal admin panel (not regular players) have login-related data recorded, such as authentication details and session information, through our login provider.

We do not use analytics or advertising trackers, and we do not collect payment/billing information, email addresses (for regular players), or location data.

As Giraê adds new features, the specific data points above may expand, but they will always fall within these categories. We'll update this policy's "Last updated" date when meaningful changes occur, rather than itemizing every individual feature.

---

## 2. How We Use This Data

We use the data we collect to:
- Operate core gameplay (collections, trades, currency, wishlists, goals, and related features)
- Display your profile and activity to yourself and, unless you've enabled Privacy Mode, to other users
- Generate in-game images (e.g., profile cards, trade results)
- Detect abuse, enforce bans, and investigate disputes
- Maintain accountability for administrator actions
- Keep our systems and admin tools secure

---

## 3. Third-Party Sharing & Security

We don't sell your data or share it with advertisers. Limited technical sharing happens as part of running the service:

- **Internal services stay internal.** Components involved in generating in-game content (such as our image-rendering service) run on our own private network alongside the rest of our infrastructure. They are not exposed to the public internet and do not send your data to outside companies.
- **Storage.** Generated images (such as avatars and card renders) are hosted on our cloud storage and served back to you and other users as part of normal gameplay.
- **Telegram / Discord.** As with any bot on these platforms, Telegram or Discord can see the commands and messages you send to interact with Giraê. Their own privacy policies govern that data.
- **Security practices.** We apply security measures throughout our infrastructure — including network isolation between internal services, access controls, and audit logging of administrative actions — to protect your data from unauthorized access.

---

## 4. Your Privacy Mode

Giraê offers a **Privacy Mode** toggle that hides your profile, card collection, and wishlist from other users.

**Important:** Privacy Mode only controls what *other players* can see. It does not stop our internal, server-side record-keeping — your gameplay history and any related admin logs are still recorded regardless of this setting. We keep these records to prevent fraud, resolve disputes, and maintain game integrity.

---

## 5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing **hello@girae.io** with your Telegram user ID or bot ID. Once we verify the request, all of your data is purged from our servers, subject to the exceptions below.

**Exceptions:**
- **Legal requests.** We may retain or disclose data if required to do so by law enforcement or other legal process.
- **Administrator accounts.** If you are (or were) an administrator, records of your administrative actions may be retained after a deletion request for conduct review and accountability purposes.

---

## 6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. To make a request, contact us at **hello@girae.io**.
